Hybrid Deep Learning and Physics models
ISIR | Sorbonne Université


Hello, I'm a PhD student on Hybrid Deep Learning and Physical approaches in the MLIA team on the ISIR under the supervision of Patrick Gallinari and Nicolas Thome. My goal is to investigate the cooperation of ML and physics models to improve the state of the art in the modeling of complex physical systems described by PDEs.


I am broadly interested in developing novel hybrid models combining deep learning and physics. Anything that relates to physic-based machine learning, foundation models, operator learning for PDEs, or PINNs (and many more!) will catch my eye!

If you are interested in collaborating with me, please email me at regueiroespino[at]isir.upmc.fr
