Hello, I'm a M2 MVA student at Paris-Saclay. Here you can find some information about myself!

Nowadays, I'm doing an internship in the TAU Team from INRIA. Under the supervision of Alessandro Leite and Audrey Poinsot, I work on causal data augmentation through an adversarial machine learning viewpoint, aiming to enable partial identifiability in causal inference settings. Hopefully, next academic year I'll start a PhD under the supervision of Patrick Gallinari and Nicolas Thome.

I'm especially interested in Statistics and Machine Learning, but I also enjoy tackling problems from other math fields. Moreover, in my free time, I like to hang out with my friends to talk about different subjects like philosophy or economy. I also like to do sports and travel as a way to discover different cultures and points of view.

From 2017 to 2021 I studied for the B. Sc. in Maths at Universidade de Santiago de Compostela (USC), in my hometown. However, in my third year, I made an exchange program (Erasmus) at Sorbonne Université in Paris (Paris VI). After finishing my Bachelor's, I decided to return to France to study for an engineering diploma in the École Nationale Supérieure de l'Informatique pour l'Industrie et l'Entreprise (ENSIIE). At the same time, I followed an M1 of Applied Maths at Université Paris-Saclay.
